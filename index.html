Weaving Heritage through Genealogy: Exploring the Lineage of Basket Makers

Techniques for weaving baskets are passed down from teacher to students and are often contained within a single family. In some instances, families own rights to specific designs and patterns. This Exhibit features makers and their respective works. Makers can often identify a specific teacher through their work and can tell which teacher taught a maker based on the similarity in design.

Yvonne Walker
